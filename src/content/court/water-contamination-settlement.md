Parties announced a $3.2 billion class action settlement today in a water contamination case affecting hundreds of thousands of residents across multiple states.

## Settlement Terms

The agreement provides:

- **Direct Compensation**: Payments based on exposure duration and health impacts
- **Medical Monitoring**: 30-year healthcare monitoring program for affected residents
- **Water Infrastructure**: $800 million for filtration and treatment systems
- **Environmental Remediation**: Cleanup of contamination sources

## Class Certification

The court certified a class of approximately 450,000 affected residents, with settlement notices beginning next month.
