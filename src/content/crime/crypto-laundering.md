The founder of a major cryptocurrency exchange was arrested today on charges of operating an unlicensed money transmitting business and laundering billions in criminal proceeds.

## Criminal Allegations

The indictment alleges:

- **Ransomware Processing**: $2 billion from ransomware attacks
- **Darknet Markets**: $1.5 billion from illegal marketplace transactions
- **Fraud Proceeds**: $800 million from various fraud schemes
- **Sanctions Evasion**: Transactions with sanctioned entities

## Platform Seizure

Authorities simultaneously seized the exchange's domain and froze approximately $700 million in assets across multiple jurisdictions.
