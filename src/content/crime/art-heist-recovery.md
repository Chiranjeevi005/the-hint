The FBI Art Crime Team announced the recovery of multiple stolen masterworks today, concluding a three-year international investigation into a sophisticated art theft ring.

## Recovered Works

The investigation led to the recovery of:

- **Impressionist Paintings**: Three works by Monet and Renoir
- **Modern Art**: Two Picasso drawings and a Kandinsky painting
- **Old Masters**: Dutch Golden Age works from private collections
- **Antiquities**: Roman and Greek archaeological artifacts

## International Cooperation

The investigation involved coordination with law enforcement agencies in seven countries, tracking the artworks through black market networks.
