The Justice Department released comprehensive data today showing significant reductions in gang-related violence in cities implementing targeted intervention programs.

## Program Results

The focused deterrence initiative achieved:

- **Shooting Reduction**: 40% decrease in gang-related shootings
- **Homicide Prevention**: 35% reduction in gang-related killings
- **Social Services**: 2,500 individuals received intervention services
- **Community Engagement**: Enhanced trust between police and communities

## Program Expansion

Based on the results, the department announced expansion of the program to 25 additional cities next year.
