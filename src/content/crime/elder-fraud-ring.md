Federal prosecutors unveiled charges today against 23 defendants in a sprawling elder fraud scheme that allegedly stole over $150 million from thousands of senior citizens nationwide.

## Scheme Operation

The fraud operation involved:

- **Romance Scams**: Fake online relationships to extract money
- **Investment Fraud**: Bogus cryptocurrency and precious metals schemes
- **Technical Support Scams**: False computer repair charges
- **Money Laundering**: International networks to move stolen funds

## Victim Impact

Investigators identified over 4,000 victims across all 50 states, with individual losses ranging from thousands to over $1 million.
