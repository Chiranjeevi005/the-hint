Federal authorities announced the dismantling of a major drug trafficking organization today, culminating in arrests across a dozen states and the largest single-day drug seizure of the year.

## Operation Details

The coordinated operation resulted in:

- **Arrests**: 47 individuals facing federal charges
- **Seizures**: 500 kilograms of fentanyl, 2 tons of methamphetamine
- **Asset Forfeiture**: $35 million in cash, multiple properties and vehicles
- **Network Disruption**: Complete shutdown of distribution infrastructure

## Investigation History

The investigation spanned 18 months, involving cooperation between federal, state, and international law enforcement agencies.
