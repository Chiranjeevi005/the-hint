Federal authorities announced the rescue of 89 trafficking victims and the arrest of 31 alleged traffickers in a coordinated operation spanning multiple southwestern cities.

## Operation Results

The enforcement action achieved:

- **Victims Rescued**: 89 individuals, including 23 minors
- **Arrests**: 31 suspected traffickers facing federal charges
- **Networks Disrupted**: Three major trafficking organizations
- **Victim Services**: Immediate access to shelter and support services

## Ongoing Mission

Authorities emphasized the operation represents ongoing efforts to combat trafficking networks and prioritize victim identification and support.
