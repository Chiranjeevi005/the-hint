Federal prosecutors announced charges against multiple companies and individuals today in connection with a large-scale illegal hazardous waste dumping operation.

## Investigation Findings

The investigation revealed:

- **Dumping Sites**: 30 locations contaminated with industrial chemicals
- **Corporate Defendants**: Five waste management companies
- **Individual Defendants**: 12 company executives and employees
- **Cleanup Costs**: Estimated $200 million for remediation

## Environmental Impact

EPA officials described significant groundwater contamination affecting multiple communities, with ongoing assessment of health impacts.
