Six Middle Eastern nations signed a landmark water-sharing agreement today, concluding negotiations that began over a decade ago amid growing concerns about regional water scarcity.

## Agreement Structure

The accord establishes:

- **Desalination Network**: Regional facilities serving multiple nations
- **Allocation Framework**: Equitable water rights distribution system
- **Conservation Standards**: Shared efficiency and reuse requirements
- **Dispute Mechanism**: Binding arbitration for allocation conflicts

## Climate Adaptation

The agreement is widely viewed as a model for climate adaptation cooperation in water-stressed regions worldwide.
