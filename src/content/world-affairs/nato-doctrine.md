NATO members endorsed a comprehensive new strategic concept today, updating the alliance's doctrine to address 21st-century security challenges.

## Doctrinal Priorities

The new concept addresses:

- **Hybrid Threats**: Enhanced response to information warfare and sabotage
- **Space Domain**: Recognition of space as operational domain
- **Climate Security**: Climate change as threat multiplier
- **Technology Competition**: Emphasis on emerging and disruptive technologies

## Alliance Commitments

Members reaffirmed the 2% GDP defense spending target while announcing new collective investment programs.
