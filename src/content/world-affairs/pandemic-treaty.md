Member states of the World Health Organization adopted a binding pandemic preparedness treaty today, establishing unprecedented international coordination mechanisms for future health emergencies.

## Treaty Provisions

The framework includes:

- **Pathogen Sharing**: Mandatory reporting and sample sharing protocols
- **Vaccine Access**: Equitable distribution requirements for affected regions
- **Early Warning**: Global surveillance network with real-time data sharing
- **Manufacturing**: Regional production capacity development commitments

## Lessons Applied

The treaty codifies lessons from COVID-19, addressing gaps in the international response that prolonged the pandemic.
