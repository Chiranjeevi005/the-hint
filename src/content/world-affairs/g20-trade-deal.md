World leaders concluded the G20 Summit in Berlin today with a landmark trade agreement that promises to reshape global commerce in clean energy and digital sectors.

## Agreement Highlights

The accord includes:

- **Tariff Reductions**: 50% cuts on solar panels, batteries, and EV components
- **Digital Standards**: Common framework for cross-border data flows
- **Dispute Resolution**: Enhanced arbitration mechanisms
- **Developing Nations**: Special provisions for emerging economies

## Global Implications

Trade analysts predict the agreement will accelerate the global transition to renewable energy while establishing long-contested digital trade rules.
