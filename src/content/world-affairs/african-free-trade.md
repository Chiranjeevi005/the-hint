The African Continental Free Trade Area officially began full implementation today, marking a historic milestone in the continent's economic integration efforts.

## Implementation Milestones

The launch includes:

- **Tariff Phase-Outs**: Elimination of duties on 90% of goods
- **Services Liberalization**: Initial commitments in key service sectors
- **Rules of Origin**: Harmonized standards enabling preferential treatment
- **Digital Integration**: Pan-African payment systems activation

## Economic Projections

Economists estimate the trade area could increase intra-African trade by 52% by 2030, creating millions of jobs across the continent.
