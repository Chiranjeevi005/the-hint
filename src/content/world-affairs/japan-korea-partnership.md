Japan and South Korea announced a comprehensive security partnership today, marking the most significant improvement in bilateral relations in decades.

## Partnership Framework

The agreement establishes:

- **Intelligence Sharing**: Real-time missile early warning data exchange
- **Joint Exercises**: Expanded trilateral coordination with the United States
- **Defense Industry Cooperation**: Joint development of regional defense systems
- **Historical Reconciliation**: New foundation for addressing wartime legacy issues

## Strategic Significance

Analysts describe the partnership as a transformative development for regional security architecture.
