India and Brazil signed a comprehensive cooperation agreement today during the Brazilian President's state visit to New Delhi, expanding partnership across strategic sectors.

## Cooperation Areas

The agreement covers:

- **Pharmaceuticals**: Joint manufacturing of generic medicines
- **Agriculture**: Technology sharing for tropical farming
- **Renewable Energy**: Solar and biofuel development cooperation
- **Defense**: Naval exercises and equipment co-production

## Trade Expansion

Both nations committed to doubling bilateral trade within five years through targeted investment facilitation.
