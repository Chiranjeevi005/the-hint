Arctic Council member states concluded negotiations today on a comprehensive resource governance framework, addressing growing concerns about development in the rapidly warming polar region.

## Framework Elements

The agreement includes:

- **Protected Areas**: Expanded marine protection zones
- **Shipping Standards**: Enhanced environmental and safety requirements
- **Resource Extraction**: Strict environmental protection for mining and drilling
- **Indigenous Rights**: Meaningful consultation requirements

## Climate Context

The framework acknowledges the Arctic is warming at four times the global rate, requiring urgent governance updates.
