The UN Security Council unanimously approved a resolution today expanding peacekeeping operations in the Sahel region, authorizing additional forces and broadening the mission's mandate.

## Mission Expansion

The resolution includes:

- **Troop Increase**: Additional 5,000 personnel from contributing nations
- **Mandate Expansion**: Authority protecting humanitarian corridors
- **Equipment Upgrades**: Enhanced surveillance and mobility assets
- **Civilian Protection**: Strengthened protection of civilians mandate

## Regional Context

The expansion comes amid escalating humanitarian concerns and persistent security challenges across the Sahel.
