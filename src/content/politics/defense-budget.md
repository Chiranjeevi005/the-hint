The House Armed Services Committee advanced the annual defense authorization bill today, approving an $886 billion package that represents the largest defense budget in American history.

## Spending Priorities

The legislation prioritizes:

- **Pacific Deterrence**: $12 billion for regional force posture
- **Nuclear Modernization**: Continued investment in deterrent forces
- **Cyber Capabilities**: Enhanced offensive and defensive operations
- **Personnel**: 4.5% military pay raise

## Strategic Focus

Committee leadership emphasized the bill's focus on great power competition and technological superiority.
