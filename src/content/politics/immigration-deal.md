Congressional leaders from both chambers announced a framework agreement on comprehensive immigration reform today, marking the most significant progress on the issue in over a decade.

## Framework Elements

The agreement outlines:

- **Border Security**: Enhanced technology and personnel investments
- **Pathway Program**: Earned citizenship process for long-term residents
- **Visa Modernization**: Employment-based visa system reforms
- **Family Reunification**: Backlog reduction measures

## Legislative Timeline

Leaders expressed confidence in advancing the legislation through both chambers before the August recess.
