Governors from 28 states signed a landmark interstate commerce agreement today, establishing new frameworks for supply chain coordination and emergency resource sharing.

## Agreement Highlights

The compact includes provisions for:

- **Supply Chain Coordination**: Shared logistics planning during emergencies
- **Regulatory Harmonization**: Streamlined permitting for cross-state transport
- **Resource Sharing**: Mutual aid agreements for critical materials
- **Digital Infrastructure**: Common standards for trade documentation

## Bipartisan Support

The agreement notably bridges partisan divides, with governors from both parties citing economic pragmatism as the driving force.
