A bipartisan group of lawmakers unveiled their long-awaited tax reform proposal today, presenting a comprehensive overhaul that aims to simplify the tax code while addressing revenue concerns.

## Major Changes

The proposal includes significant reforms:

- **Bracket Consolidation**: Reduces individual tax brackets from seven to four
- **Corporate Minimum Tax**: Establishes 15% minimum for large corporations
- **Carbon Pricing**: Introduces $50 per ton carbon fee with border adjustments
- **Deduction Reform**: Caps itemized deductions for high earners

## Economic Projections

Economists project the plan would generate $1.2 trillion in new revenue over a decade while maintaining progressivity.
