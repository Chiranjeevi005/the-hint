The Department of Education announced a major funding initiative today, directing $45 billion toward rural and underserved school districts over the next five years.

## Program Components

The initiative includes:

- **Infrastructure Modernization**: Building renovations and technology upgrades
- **Teacher Recruitment**: Loan forgiveness and salary supplements for rural educators
- **Digital Access**: Broadband connectivity for remote learning
- **Curriculum Support**: STEM and vocational program development

## Implementation Timeline

Funding distribution begins in the new fiscal year, with priority given to districts demonstrating acute resource needs.
