The President announced sweeping changes to the Cabinet today, with three senior officials set to depart in what observers describe as a significant strategic realignment.

## Departures Confirmed

The following officials will leave their posts:

- Secretary of Commerce
- Secretary of Energy  
- Director of the Office of Management and Budget

## New Appointments

The administration has nominated experienced professionals to fill the vacancies, emphasizing continuity in policy direction while bringing fresh perspectives to implementation strategies.
