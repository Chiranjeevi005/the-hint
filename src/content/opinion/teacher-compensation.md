Teacher salaries have declined in real terms for two decades. Meanwhile, we ask more of teachers than ever before—not just instruction, but counseling, safety, and social-emotional support.

## The Talent Crisis

Talented graduates increasingly choose other professions. Education programs struggle to fill seats. Experienced teachers leave the profession in record numbers.

## An Investment, Not an Expense

Raising teacher compensation is not simply a matter of fairness—it is an investment in our collective future. Countries that pay teachers well consistently outperform those that do not.

The correlation could not be clearer. The choice could not be more obvious.
