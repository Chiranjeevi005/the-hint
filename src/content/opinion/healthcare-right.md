Every developed nation except the United States provides universal healthcare coverage to its citizens. This is not a matter of ideology but of basic human dignity.

## The Moral Imperative

No person should die because they cannot afford insulin. No family should face bankruptcy because of a cancer diagnosis. No child should go without treatment because their parents lack insurance.

## Beyond Politics
